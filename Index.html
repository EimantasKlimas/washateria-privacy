Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 23, 2026

Washateria ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled.

No Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data with the developer or third parties.

All processing occurs locally on your device or within your personal iCloud account.

Camera and Photo Library Access

The App may request access to your camera or photo library to allow you to capture or select images of clothing care labels.

These images are processed entirely on your device. They are not transmitted to the developer or any external servers.

iCloud Sync

The App uses Apple iCloud to synchronize your data across your devices.

This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is managed by Apple. The developer does not have access to this data.

For more information, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Support Contact

If you contact support via email, your email address and message will be used solely to respond to your inquiry.

Your email will not be used for marketing or shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Data Security

Your data remains under your control and is protected by Apple's security systems and your device security.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Changes will be reflected on this page.
